CoMotion is a student-led motion graphics conference that brings together top industry professionals with Motion Media Design students at the Savannah College of Art and Design that includes a keynote speech, panel discussions, the annual curated and juried Student Showcase,
1-on-1 portfolio reviews, and an award ceremony.

CoMotion 2023 will be held on March 10th & 11th, 2023.
More information on the website will be updated through February.
