Kirsten Noll

Executive Producer, Scholar

Kirsten is the Managing Executive Producer at Scholar in Los Angeles. With over a decade of creative production experience, she is passionate about design-driven and multidisciplinary narratives. Her diverse background in live-action, design/animation, and VFX means she's seen it all. As the media landscape continues to shift, Kirsten seeks to provide mentorship and an inclusive environment within the creative production process.
